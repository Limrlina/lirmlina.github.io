About me

Hi, my name is Milana and I am a beginner illustrator. Since my childhood I have been drawing and I plan to dedicate my life to it (or at least try to). I took a course at SkillsUp School in basic drawing and attended a lot of workshops, such as "Casual 2D Render", "Portrait Sketching Step-by-Step", "Figure Sketching" and many others. Also I am a student of Saint-Petersburg Polytechnic University, where I study Applied Informatics. At the moment I am working on website layout and prototyping.
